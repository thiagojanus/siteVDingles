Pedro Souza

Pedro Lucas De Souza, born on September 16, 2006, is a 16 year old student at Etec Maria Cristina Medeiros (Etec MCM) in Mauá. Currently, he is studying a Computer Technician for the Internet and demonstrates a great passion for technology and its vast online applications. Furthermore, since 2021, he has been committed to improving his English language skills by attending the "Ls Idiomas" school in Ribeirão Pires.

In his spare time, Pedro is a football enthusiast, practices and watches matches regularly. He is also an avid video game player, with a special penchant for "FIFA". His passion for films and series led him to choose "Dark", a German science fiction series, as his favorite, due to the complex and intriguing narrative.

Although he was born in Mauá, Pedro moved to Ribeirão Pires at the age of 9, in 2016, a move that enriched his life with new experiences and opportunities. Overall, Pedro is a multifaceted young man, with interests that include technology, languages, sports and pop culture, promising a bright future full of possibilities as he continues to grow in Ribeirão Pires and beyond.
